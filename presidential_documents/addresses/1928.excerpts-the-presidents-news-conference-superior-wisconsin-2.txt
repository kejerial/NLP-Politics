1928 Excerpts The Presidents News Conference Superior Wisconsin 2

I am expecting to go over to Duluth some day. I think they are planning a sort of a drive for me around there. I don't know just when I shall go. I have been so busy out at the Lodge catching fish— there are 45,000 out there—I haven't caught them all yet, but I have them all pretty well intimidated. They have had to restock one lake.
